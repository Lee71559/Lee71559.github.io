Minesweeper Equation #1

April 28, 2021

Theory

Let X and Y be 2 known numbers, and:
A = number of grids in area A
B = number of grids in area B
X = number in grid X
Y = number in grid Y

If Y = X + B, then area B is full of mines, area A has no mines.
